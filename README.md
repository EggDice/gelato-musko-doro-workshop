## Materials

All materials are provided by Musko:

 - 2 cups whole milk
 - 2 whole eggs
 - 1 cup white sugar
 - 1/3 cup cocoa powder
 - 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
 - Medium bag of ice
 - 1/2 cup Kosher salt

Please let Musko know, what flavour of Gelato would you like to do, so he can
buy the ingredients.
